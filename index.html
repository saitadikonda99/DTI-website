Laser Scarecrow Project

The Laser Scarecrow project is a new approach to bird deterrence in agriculture, aiming to improve on the limitations of traditional scarecrows. The Laser Scarecrow uses lasers and sensors to detect and deter birds from crops, providing a more effective and sustainable solution.
